Featured Case Study

How I Identified 3 Key Churn Drivers to Improve Customer Retention

Step 01
Problem
Business was losing customers with no clear understanding of why. Needed to identify patterns in churned vs. retained customers.
Step 02
Approach
Cleaned and queried customer data using SQL. Segmented customers by behaviour, demographics, and transaction history.
Step 03
Analysis
Built a Power BI dashboard with DAX measures tracking churn rate, customer lifetime value, and segment-level KPIs.
Step 04
Result
Pinpointed 3 root causes of churn. Delivered recommendations adopted by the retention team for strategic action.
